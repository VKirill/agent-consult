# Support Policy

Thank you for using **Agent Consult**! If you encounter any bugs, have questions, or need assistance, here is how you can get support.

---

## 🔍 1. Check the Documentation First

Before seeking support, please review the files in the `docs/` folder:
* **Troubleshooting**: [docs/troubleshooting.en.md](file:///home/ubuntu/mcp_server/agent_counsult/docs/troubleshooting.en.md) (or [docs/troubleshooting.md](file:///home/ubuntu/mcp_server/agent_counsult/docs/troubleshooting.md) in Russian) covers common setups, logging, and timeouts.
* **Architecture**: [docs/architecture.en.md](file:///home/ubuntu/mcp_server/agent_counsult/docs/architecture.en.md) describes the internals and sandbox directory structure.

---

## 💬 2. Community & Chat

For general questions, discussions, or sharing your multi-agent prompts:
* **Telegram Channel**: Join [t.me/pomogay_marketing](https://t.me/pomogay_marketing) to connect with the author and other users.
* **GitHub Discussions**: Visit the [Discussions tab](https://github.com/VKirill/agent-consult/discussions) on our repository if enabled.

---

## 🐛 3. Report a Bug

If you found a bug in the code (e.g. script errors, config parsing errors, process cleanup failures):
1. Go to the [Issues Tab](https://github.com/VKirill/agent-consult/issues).
2. Click **New Issue** -> **Bug Report**.
3. Fill out the issue template, providing logs, steps to reproduce, and your environment version details (Node.js, OS).

---

## 💡 4. Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new consultant role or a new feature?
1. Open an issue under the [Issues Tab](https://github.com/VKirill/agent-consult/issues).
2. Choose **Feature Request**.
3. Describe the feature, why it is needed, and any potential implementation details.
